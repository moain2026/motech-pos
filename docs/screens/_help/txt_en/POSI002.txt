Additional Keypad 
 > 
 POS System > System Inputs > 
 Additional Keypad 
 Use: This screen is 
used for coding the additional keypads, and it is used in the Sales Invoices 
screen. For example, a keypad for the items that do not have a barcode is added 
here and is linked to its items from the Keypad Items 
screen. 
 How to Use the Screen 
 After clicking on the Add button, the screen is used as 
follows: 
 - 
 Additional 
Keypad Number: This field is 
used to register the additional keypad number, noting that each POS can be 
linked to the additional keypad in the POS Devices and Machines 
screen. 
 - 
 Additional 
Keypad Name/ Foreign Name: The keypad name 
is added here in the system default language, as well as, the foreign language. 
Note that the keypad’s foreign name will appear upon searching for an item that 
is linked to it in the Sales Invoices screen in order to distinguish between the 
different keypads that will be added. 
 - 
 Save: Users press 
this button after completing data 
entry.
