 Link Invoice to Loyalty Program Customer 
 Use: This screen is 
used to link the loyalty program customer to a certain sales invoice to which 
the customer has not been linked before. This is done based on a sales invoice 
through the cashier; in order to allow adding new points to the customer and 
calculating them in the loyalty program. 
 How to Use Screen 
 This screen appears upon adding an invoice, which is explained as 
follows: 
 - 
 Invoice 
Number: This field is 
used to enter the invoice number. 
 - 
 Invoice 
Date/Time/Currency/Exchange Rate: The data of 
these fields appear automatically once the user enters the invoice number and 
clicks ENTER in the keypad. 
 - 
 Detailed 
Data: The detailed 
data of the items that have been sold to the customer appear after selecting the 
invoice number. 
 - 
 Mobile 
Number/ Customer Number / Customer Name: The customer 
for whom the points will be calculated on the selected invoice is entered here. 
Once the customer number or mobile number is entered, the rest of the fields 
will appear automatically. 
 - 
 Number 
of Points: The number of 
points that will be calculated for the customer on the selected invoice appears 
here. 
 - 
 Save: Users press 
this button after completing data 
entry.
